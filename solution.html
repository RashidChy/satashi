The Solution

In the era of booming technology,why the system is not fully automated. So what exactly are we trying to implement to solve this problem? Added two extra layers of existing system upload necessary documents.

Our Solution

By fully automating the verification process, our system saves valuable time and energy of our country's citizens. Sir Benjamin Franklin once said, time is money. So by saving time, our tech vision will be generating money and take us one step closer towards creating digital Bangladesh. 

  • We added two extra layers of existing system upload necessary documents. 

    1. Upload verification documents.
    2. Book an appointment for the biometric verification.

    When applicants upload the documents, a regional officer examines the documents thoroughly and may verify or reject the application. Application is successfully verified. 

  • Fix the desired schedule to go through the biometric verification

    Civilians can fix the desired schedule to go through the biometric verification process physically at their nearest passport office at their expected time. Breaking the convention of waiting for long hours in a long queue. 

  • No more Queues

    The proposed system greatly helps our country senior citizens and job holders. Senior citizens don't have to wait in long queues and job holders don't have to take leave from the office. Systems main benefit is that more applications can be processed daily.
