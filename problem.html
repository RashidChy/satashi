The problem



Passport or national documents in our country is a big hassle. Even today, citizens must stand in long queues for hours to submit applications and to give biometric identification. 

Long queues and lack of proper seating arrangements have turned passport offices all over Bangladesh into a nightmare for elderly people and with physical disabilities. 

There are:

  • over 60 regional passport offices in the country
  • 30,000 applications are filled daily
  • Sadly, only one third of the applications go through the final biometric verification.

The existing system,

was announced in 2019 by the government to mitigate all the problems with the manual method and make the system fully automated. 

The government attempted a mega transformation in a passport operation by introducing the online application system and a passport. It was to be a new era of hassle-free and speedy service where one could submit hard copy of online application form, but then had to provide biometric data and other documents physically. The new era, however, has only been on papers. Citizens can now complete their applications online, but they must still stand in long queues for ours to submit applications and give biometric identification.
